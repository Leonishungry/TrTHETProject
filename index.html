About This Project

GreenScape Myanmar is a small website project designed to practice web fundamentals while promoting environmental awareness. Students will write their own short paragraphs and add images related to local green initiatives.

Please replace the sample text below with your group’s original writing: why green spaces matter, how communities can help, and one practical step we can do this week.

Our Project Ideas

Tree Planting

Organize a small tree planting day. Document the steps and share care tips for the first three months.

Idea
Clean-up Day

Plan a safe litter clean-up around a small area. Separate recyclables and note how many bags were collected.

Idea
Bench and Shade

Design a simple resting area sketch with benches and shade. Ask for feedback from neighbors and improve the plan.
